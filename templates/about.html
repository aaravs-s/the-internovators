About SafeWalkers

Safer route planning for everyday movement.

SafeWalkers is a student-built prototype for comparing walking and biking routes with safety, comfort, and local context in mind.

The goal is simple: help people make more confident travel choices by combining route options, safety scoring, saved-route notes, and community knowledge in one lightweight app.

Built for Austin first

Designed around city walking, campus trips, errands, commutes, and short rides.
